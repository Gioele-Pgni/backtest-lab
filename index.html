EasyLanguage Strategy Collection

A repository of trading strategies written in EasyLanguage for TradeStation and MultiCharts. It includes implementations of Trend Following, Mean Reversion, Breakout, and statistical models, designed to provide a solid foundation for the development and backtesting of quantitative algorithms.

Strategies

  • Trend Following with ATR
  • Mean Reversion with Bollinger Bands
  • Breakout with Keltner Channels
