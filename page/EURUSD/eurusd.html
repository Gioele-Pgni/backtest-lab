EURUSD

EUR/USD (fibra) – Most traded pair in the world (~25% of forex volume). Highest liquidity and volatility during the London–New York overlap (13:00–17:00 GMT). Average daily range (ADR) 70–90 pips under normal conditions. Key events: Non-Farm Payrolls, FOMC rate decision, ECB rate decision.

As evidenced by empirical analysis, the instrument's price dynamics alternate systematically between clearly defined directional trends and prolonged phases of lateral consolidation. This behavioral configuration presents substantial scope for the implementation of advanced algorithmic solutions. Specifically, the development roadmap encompasses the adoption of a trend-following model, engineered to capitalize on the high and low levels recorded during the previous session. From an operational management and systemic risk mitigation perspective, the operational choice entails the systematic liquidation of all open positions on Friday, prior to the official closing of the financial markets.
Preliminary testing of the strategy in its native form has yielded promising results. Despite the most challenging market phases, the equity line has maintained a consistently upward trajectory, confirming the resilience of the underlying algorithmic model. This virtuous behavior provides solid scientific validation, encouraging us to proceed with enthusiasm towards the next phases of development. The strategy demonstrates this intrinsic value even before the implementation of proprietary filters and advanced risk management protocols, elements that will further increase its stability and efficiency.
